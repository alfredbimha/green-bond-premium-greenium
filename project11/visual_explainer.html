Understanding the Green Bond Premium (Greenium)

Do investors accept lower yields on green bonds? And how do we measure it rigorously?

What Is a Greenium?

If investors value the "green" label on a bond, they'll accept a slightly lower yield — paying a premium for sustainability. This yield discount is the greenium.

Greenium = Yield(conventional bond) − Yield(matched green bond)

Typical estimates: 2–15 basis points (0.02% to 0.15%). Small, but significant for large issuances.
